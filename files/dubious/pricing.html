FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Let us help answer the most common questions you might have.

Will my users know that I'm using Dubious?

For all free accounts, we include a small "Powered by Dubious" link at the bottom of all emails sent. You can easily remove this on any of our paid plans.

Is there a money back guarantee?

Yup! We offer a 15-day money back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're at all unhappy with Dubious 15 days after your first purchase, we'll happily refund your payment...and even stay friends!

Does the free trial require a credit card up front?

Nope! Every paid plan also comes with a 30 day free trial with no credit card required up front.

Does it work with Rails? PHP? Node?

Yes! Dubious is super easy to implement across all major server-side languages. Learn more about integrating Dubious.

Does Dubious store personally identifiable information (PII) about my users or customers?

By default, we don't directly store any information about your users. We use Postmark for faciliting suspicious login emails and they will store a record of your users email. They are extremely secure and GDPR compliant. If you haven't heard of Postmark, they've been around for over a decade and are used by major brands like IKEA, Asana, Moz, 1Password, just to name a few!

Can I control how many emails get sent to my users?

You sure can! We offer 3 levels of security that will control when suspicious login emails get sent to your users. Learn more about login detection.
